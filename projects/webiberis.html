UI Design

Project Overview

The goal of this project was to redesign the website of my client Mr. Ashok Prajapati, founder of WebiBeris. The purpose of the overhaul was to give the business a new modern look and a better readability to the website. Though broadly classified as a UI Design project, I played several roles from start to finish which included strategizing and writing content along with designing visuals and the interface of the website.

...

Navigation

To begin with, I analyzed the content that was present on the existing website and the way that it was presented. Based on this and the objective of the business, I was able to finalize the content, group the content into categories and the order in which it should be presented. This was helpful in determining the navigation of the page.

  • Home - to provide an impactful overview of the business

  • Features - to walk clients through the supported features

  • Services - to provide info about various pricing options

  • Work - to talk about the amazing projects accomplished

  • Clients - feedback from clients for more credibility

  • About - to share the business' history

  • Contact - for clients to get a quote for their project

Wireframe

Once the navigation content was finalized, I began to sketch out wireframes for the layout. Sketching the wireframe was helpful as it allowed me to layout the content of the website and also provided me an opportunity to try different layouts without utilizing more time.

Design Style Guide

After getting the final wireframe approved, I began with the high-fidelity mockup of the design. Approaching it the minimal way, I restricted my color palette to whites, blacks, and gradients of blue. I combined the sparing usage of colors with ample negative space and made use of clean and modern typography.

Illustrations
